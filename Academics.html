ESKİŞEHİR TECHNICAL UNIVERSITY

FACULTIES

Faculty of Science Faculty of Architecture and Design Faculty of Engineering Faculty of Sports Sciences Faculty of Aeronautics and Space Sciences
Eskişehir Technical University Faculty of Science has adopted the vision of being a pioneer that generates knowledge that will be the key to scientific innovations and provides education to society by using all kinds of media The goal of the Faculty of Architecture and Design is to train individuals who have foresight and universal values, in addition to individuals who think analytically, while designing creative and innovative products , have professional responsibilities and self-confidence. In all the departments of the faculty, 4-year-undergraduate education programmes are offered following a one-year English-preparatory instruction. All the departments are equipped with education and research laboratories. Numerous research and development projects are conducted in all the departments of the faculty. Practical courses and sports activities in the faculty are conducted in the multi purpose halls, having a total indoor area of 66089 m² (basketball, handball, volleyball, gym, artistic gymnastics hall, rhythmic gymnastics hall, badminton hall, table tennis hall, folk dances and wrestling hall, fencing hall, massage parlor, fitness, fitness hall, squash hall) The aim of the faculty is to train qualified aviation staff for the sector in the fields of air traffic control, aviation management, pilotage and aircraft maintenance in accordance with international aviation standards and air transport rules. In addition to flight training activities, the Faculty runs the International Eskişehir Hasan Polatkan Airport, where domestic and international flights take place.

INSTITUTES


Institution of Graduate Schools Institute Of Transportation and Communication Sciences Earth and Space Sciences Institute
It is to train researchers in the relevant field through postgraduate education, who can use universal knowledge, skills and values and prioritize producing usable outputs by adding new ones that are needed by the society with all clusters. The Transportation Economics Research Institute was established within Anadolu University in 1993 with the Decree Law No. 496, affiliated to the Rectorate. The Institute of Transportation Sciences is the first and only institute in its field. The main purpose of our institute is to obtain the necessary information and analyzes for the discovery of human settlements that do not threaten nature and are not threatened by nature.

VOCATIONAL SCHOOLS


Porsuk Vocational School Vocational School of Transportation
Porsuk Vocational School located in Eskişehir city center; As a result of the major maintenance and repair of the three-storey, three-block building that previously belonged to the General Directorate of TCDD, it started education in the 2001-2002 academic year. Eskişehir Technical University Transportation Vocational School started its education and training activities in 2012-2013 academic year by incorporating 9 Programs within Anadolu University Porsuk Vocational School.
